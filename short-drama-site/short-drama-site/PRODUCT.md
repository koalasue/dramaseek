# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Chinese-speaking viewers searching for overseas vertical short dramas, plus administrators managing verified sources. They use phones and desktop browsers and need to locate full episodes quickly without wading through recaps, trailers, reactions, or unrelated videos.

## Product Purpose

Search overseas short-drama titles across supported platforms, preserve original titles, group results by source, and distinguish live platform candidates from verified official resources. Provide a separate download workflow only for direct media files the user owns or is authorized to save.

## Brand Personality

Direct, trustworthy, efficient. The interface should feel like a focused utility rather than an entertainment portal or marketing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic streaming-homepage carousels, neon media dashboards, excessive cards, unclear source provenance, fake result counts, and interfaces that treat commentary or trailers as full drama resources.

## Design Principles

- Put search and source verification before decoration.
- Preserve the title language the user searched with.
- Never imply an unavailable connector has searched a platform.
- Make the two core jobs, search and authorized download, immediately visible.
- Prefer honest empty states and explicit safety boundaries over fabricated completeness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, full keyboard navigation, clear focus states, reduced-motion support, readable Chinese and English typography, and responsive layouts from narrow mobile screens to desktop displays.
